About CDCI Global Inc.

Your Creative Partner for Positive Global Impact

CDCI Global Inc. (formerly CDC International Consulting, LLC) is a US-based corporation with global outreach. Established in 2016, CDCI has successfully executed projects across Africa, specializing in management consulting and technical assistance.

With 18 years of experience, we have worked with major development agencies, including:

  • World Bank
  • European Union
  • USAID
  • African Development Bank
  • TradeMark East Africa
